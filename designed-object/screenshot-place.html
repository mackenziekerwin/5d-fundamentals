augmented reality

This first AR experience showcases the baseball hat in its natural state--resting on a shelf, ready to be plucked and taken out for the day. The experience contains two separate images: the background plate and the transparent hat. The background is the shelf without the hat, so when the image is brought to life, the hat rests on the foreground, and the shelf can still be seen behind it.

The Artivive app allows augmented reality experiences to be viewed right on your mobile device. To the right is a screen-recording of what happens when you try it out: simply point the camera at a picture of the artwork and watch the image come to life!
